About

The Buffalo Blitz is the University at Buffalo’s inaugural gaming festival. Despite the challenges posed by COVID-19, we seek to bring together the collegiate community in the United States Northeast in celebrate all the great things gaming has done for our communities, and to discuss how to move ourselves forward in the collegiate space.

To that end, the Buffalo Blitz will feature 8 separate gaming tournaments, and 5 panels. The event will be held virtually on March 5th – 7th. Everyone is invited to come participate, whether it be as a competitor, a speaker, or spectator!
